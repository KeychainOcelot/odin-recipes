Steak Recipe

Description

Steaks are delicious aren't they. Here's what someone who can't cook thinks what steak is supposed to look like.

I will not be held responsible for how you feel after reading and/or cooking this.

Ingredients

  • Steak cut of your choice
  • Frying pan
  • Pepper
  • Salt

Steps

Disclaimer: This is definitely not a real recipe.

  1. Throw salt and pepper on your cut of steak
  2. Throw it in a hot pan with a little bit of oil
  3. Flip it
  4. Let it rest for 10 minutes
