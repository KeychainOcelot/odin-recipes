Lasagna Recipe

Description

A lasagna is an Italian pasta-styled dish that involves baking pasta with cheese, and a tomato-based sauce.

Pastas are usually enjoyed as a meal in themselves, but often pair well with some toasted garlic bread and white wine.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef and Italian sausages
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato sauce
  • Sugar
  • Parsley
  • Basil
  • Italian seasoning
  • Oregano
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Cheese

Steps

Here's how you put everything together. This is not a real recipe please don't sue me if you try this and your oven explodes.

  • Mash the pasta
  • Layer the sausages with cheese
  • Set fire to your cheese in the oven
  • Throw in all of your seasoning, onions and garlic into the fire
  • Put some more cheese on the fire
  • Serve hot.
